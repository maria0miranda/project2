Gelly Roll

Gelly Rolls are gel-based ink pens made by Sakura, the same company that makes the popular micron pens. It brings a consistent weight and is great for layering on top of other inks. Gelly Rolls present many bright colors and shiny finishes that are hard to refuse.


I currently use the metallic set from them, which has reflective shiny finish!
