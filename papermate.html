Paper Mate

Paper Mate is your run of the mill office pen. You can find them at any supply store, but what's special to them is their range of pens: felt, gel, and ballpoint.


While they provide a good range, I mainly use their felt tip pens because they provide a even weight and is smooth to apply. They dry really quick too, which is a huge plus!
