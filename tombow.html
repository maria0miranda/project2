Tombow

You can usually spot a Tombow pen in calligraphers work, and it makes sense! Their flexible felt brush is amazing and gives you control to contrast letterforms. You can also use them for watercolors!


I use these dual brush pens for lettering, but mostly experimenting the brush to create textures and streaks.
